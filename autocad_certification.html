About My AutoCad Journey

As an undergraduate student in civil engineering, proficiency in computer-aided design (CAD) software is essential. At VIT Chennai, during my first semester, I was introduced to AutoCAD, a widely utilized tool for creating precise 3D models and building plans. Recognizing its significance in my field, I devoted substantial time to mastering AutoCAD's functionalities.

Through diligent practice and dedication, I familiarized myself with various commands and techniques essential for efficient drafting in AutoCAD. This included honing my skills in both 2D and 3D modeling, ensuring that I could accurately translate architectural concepts into digital representations.

Now, having attained a proficient level of expertise in AutoCAD, I am capable of swiftly generating building plans and 3D models with precision and efficiency. The hours invested in learning and practice have equipped me to navigate the software adeptly, allowing me to create detailed designs without the need for daily practice sessions.
