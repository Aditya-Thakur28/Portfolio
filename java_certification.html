About My Java Journey

During my second semester at VIT Chennai, I was introduced to Java programming. I found this versatile language to be rich and powerful. My learning journey involved a combination of resources, including YouTube tutorials, online articles, and courses, complemented by classroom instruction. This comprehensive approach helped me gain a solid understanding of Java.

For my end-semester project, I developed a Supermarket Inventory Management System using Java, JDBC, and MySQL. The system efficiently handled both administrative tasks and customer-side operations related to shopping and inventory management.

Currently, Java remains my primary language, and I am actively working to further strengthen my grasp of it.
